Learn about our security

X

Your security is our top priority. That's why we use a PCI-compliant data center, which uses the same secure AES 256-bit encryption as major financial institutions worldwide. We perform regular 3rd party security audits, and use RSA, Verisign and other state of the art security solutions to protect our site.

We also protect you through device identification. This controls who can access your account and on what device. Each and every device that links to your account is first authenticated by you. Before you can access your account on a new computer, tablet or smart phone, we'll give you a call or send you an email. All you need to do is confirm your identity and approve the new device.
